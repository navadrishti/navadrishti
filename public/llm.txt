# Navadrishti - Social Impact Platform

## About
Navadrishti is a comprehensive social impact platform connecting individuals, NGOs, and companies to drive positive change. We facilitate CSR initiatives, service offerings, volunteering, verification, and community engagement.

## Key Features
- Social feed for impact stories and updates
- Service requests and offers for community support
- CSR campaign management for companies
- Volunteer opportunities and tracking
- Verification system for NGOs and organizations

## Platform Structure
- User types: Individuals, NGOs, Companies
- Verification status: Verified organizations with badges
- Activity tracking: Posts, volunteering, service requests, and service offers
- Leaderboard: Top contributors and impact makers

## Main Pages
- Home: Social feed and community updates
- NGOs: Discover verified NGOs and their work
- Service Offers: Find services offered by community members
- Service Requests: Request help from the community
- CSR Campaigns: Corporate social responsibility initiatives

## Technology
Built with Next.js, React, TypeScript, and Supabase
